Important Historical Events in Israel

This table provides a summary of key events in the history of the State of Israel, including dates, significance, and brief descriptions.

Major Historical Events in Israel
Year Event Location Description
1948 Declaration of Independence Tel Aviv David Ben-Gurion declared the establishment of the State of Israel on May 14, 1948.
1967 Six-Day War All Borders Israel fought against Egypt, Syria, and Jordan, capturing Jerusalem, the West Bank, Gaza, and Golan Heights.
1973 Yom Kippur War Sinai & Golan Heights Egypt and Syria launched a surprise attack on Yom Kippur; Israel pushed back but at heavy cost.
1993 Oslo Accords Washington D.C. Peace agreement signed between Israel and the PLO, witnessed by U.S. President Bill Clinton.
2005 Gaza Disengagement Gaza Strip Israel unilaterally withdrew all military and settlements from Gaza under Ariel Sharon's plan.
