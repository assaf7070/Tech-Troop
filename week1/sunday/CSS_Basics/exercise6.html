Shakshuka

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Crack eggs on top, cover and cook until eggs are set.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with bread.
